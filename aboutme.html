Hello, I'm Kristen! A UX/UI designer. I strive to solve complex problems with creative simplicity.
I come from a backgorund in mobile and web development, graduating from Kennesaw State University with a degree in Information Technology and a minor in Computer Science.
I have also completed a Georgia Tech UX/UI bootcamp where I was able to utilize my technological background and learn valuable skills such as Qualitative & Quantitative research methodologies, Persona Creation, Storyboarding, prototyping on Figma, InVision, and Adobe XD, and more.
In my spare time, I enjoy playing golf, crocheting, painting, and spending time with my four animals!
